Snack for the Day

Dried fruits and nuts along with fresh fruits.

Ingredients

For Dried Fruits and Nuts:
  • Walnuts
  • Cashews
  • Pistachios
  • Dried cranberries
  • Dried apricots, chopped
  • Raisins or sultanas
  • Your favorite nuts or dried fruits

    • For Fresh Fruits:
  • Apple slices
  • Grapes (red or green)
  • Berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, etc.)
  • Banana slices
  • Oranges or mandarin segments

    • Recipie

    Prepare the Nuts:
  • If the nuts are raw, you can toast them in a dry skillet over medium heat for a few minutes until they become fragrant. Be careful not to burn them. Allow the nuts to cool completely.

    • Combine Dried Fruits and Nuts:
  • In a large bowl, mix together the almonds, walnuts, cashews, pistachios, dried cranberries, dried apricots, raisins, and any other dried fruits or nuts of your choice. Adjust the quantities based on your preferences.

    • Prepare Fresh Fruits:
  • Wash and prepare the fresh fruits. Cut the apples into slices, halve the grapes, slice the bananas, and segment the oranges.

    • Combine Everything:
  • Add the fresh fruit slices to the bowl with the dried fruits and nuts. Gently toss everything together to create a well-mixed combination.

    • Serve:
  • Portion the fruit and nut mix into serving bowls or individual snack containers.

    • Optional Additions:
  • For added sweetness, you can include a drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of cinnamon over the mix.

    • Enjoy:
  • Enjoy your delicious and nutritious fruit and nut mix as a snack, breakfast, or even as a topping for yogurt or oatmeal.
