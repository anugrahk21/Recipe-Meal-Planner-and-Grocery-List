Breakfast for the Day

Scrambled eggs with toast and avocado.

Ingredients

Scrambled Eggs:
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter or cooking oil
  • Optional: a splash of milk or cream for creamier eggs

    • For Toast and Avocado:
  • Slices of your favorite bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: red pepper flakes, hot sauce, or a drizzle of olive oil

    • Recipie

    Prepare the Toast:
  • slices of your favorite bread to your desired level of crispiness.

    • Prepare the Avocado:
  • While the bread is toasting, slice the ripe avocado. You can mash it or leave it in slices, depending on your preference. Season the avocado slices with salt and pepper.
    • Scramble the Eggs:
  • Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together.
  • Add a pinch of salt and pepper (and a splash of milk or cream if desired) and whisk until well combined.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or cooking oil.
  • Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them cook for a moment without stirring.Then, gently stir with a spatula, pushing the eggs from the edges toward the center.
  • Continue stirring and folding until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly creamy. Be careful not to overcook them.

    • Assemble the Dish:
  • Place the scrambled eggs on top of the toasted bread slices.

    • Add Avocado:
  • Arrange the seasoned avocado slices on top of the scrambled eggs.

    • Optional Toppings:
  • Sprinkle red pepper flakes, add hot sauce, or drizzle olive oil on top for extra flavor.

    • Serve:
  • Serve the scrambled eggs with toast and avocado immediately, while the eggs are still warm.
